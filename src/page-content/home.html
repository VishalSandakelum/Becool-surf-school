Surf in Weligama Bay – Sri Lanka’s Premier Surf Destination

Be Cool Surf School is strategically positioned in Weligama Bay, Sri Lanka, a globally celebrated surf destination famed for its gentle sandy-bottom waves—perfect for beginners—and consistent year-round swells that challenge advanced surfers. Thanks to the southwest monsoon, the bay delivers 300+ surfable days annually, ensuring optimal conditions every season. Our beachfront setup grants instant access to the waves, eliminating crowded commutes and immersing you directly in Weligama’s vibrant surf culture. Beyond the breaks, explore Sri Lanka’s southern gems: spot majestic blue whales in Mirissa (15 minutes away) or wander the UNESCO-listed Galle Fort (30 minutes west), a 17th-century Dutch colonial marvel. Here, world-class surfing meets rich cultural adventures, all framed by golden sands and palm-fringed shores.
