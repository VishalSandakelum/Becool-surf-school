Group Surfing Packages

Who Are Group Surfing Packages For?

  • Friends or families looking for a fun outdoor activity

  • Solo travelers wanting a social experience

  • School or youth groups interested in skill-building activities

  • Corporate teams seeking unique team bonding

  • Beginners and intermediates wanting structured group coaching

 

 What’s Included in the Package:

  • Professional surf instruction by certified coaches

  • High-quality equipment: surfboard, rash guard, leash

  • 90–100 minute sessions (land and water training)

  • Ocean safety and wave awareness training

  • Group coaching in small-to-medium class sizes (maintained for safety and quality)

  • Feedback and progress tips for each participant

  • Optional multi-day packages for continued learning

 

Lesson Format Overview

  1. Introduction & Equipment Setup
    Meet your instructor, get matched with the right board, and warm up with your group.

  2. Land-Based Training
    Learn surfing basics: paddling, popping up, stance, balance, and safety.

  3. Ocean Awareness & Safety Briefing
    Understand tides, wave types, current positions, and how to fall safely.

  4. Water Time – Catch Waves Together
    Head into the whitewater zone with your group. Instructors assist each surfer and provide tips in real-time as you catch and ride your waves.

  5. Wrap-Up & Review
    Cool down on the beach, share the stoke, and receive group and individual feedback.

 

Benefits of Group Surf Lessons

  • Affordable and value-packed

  • Encourages group bonding and teamwork

  • Great for photos and shared memories

  • Safe and supportive learning environment

  • Flexible group sizes and private group options available

 

Booking Options

We offer:

  • Single-day group sessions

  • Multi-day group surf packages

  • Custom group packages for schools, tours, and retreats

Just tell us your group size and goals, and we’ll arrange the perfect session.

ABOUT BECOOL

Be Cool Surf School is a surf school dedicated to teaching surfing in a fun, safe, and supportive environment. Located in a popular surf destination, the school offers lessons for all skill levels, from beginners to advanced surfers.
